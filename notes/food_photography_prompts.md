# Food Photography Prompts for Casa

This document contains the prompt structure used to generate the meal images for the Casa Kitchen Demo.

## Chosen Style
**Bright & Clean with Close-up Crop (Hybrid of Option 1 and Option 3)**

### General Prompt Formula:
To recreate this style for new meals, use the following base prompt and replace the `[FOOD ITEM]` and `[SPECIFICS]` tags.

`Close-up macro food photography of [FOOD ITEM WITH SPECIFICS], filling the entire frame. Shot from top-down perspective, bright natural light, soft shadows. Clean white marble countertop background visible only at the very edges, minimalist modern styling, 85mm lens, f/2.8, highly detailed, photorealistic.`
